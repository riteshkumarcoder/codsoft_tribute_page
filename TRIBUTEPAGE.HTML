Swami Vivekanand

A Great Indian that present india in front of whole world .

About the Legend

*Early Life: Born on January 12, 1863 in a wealthy Bengali family. His father ,Vishwanath Datta,was a lawyer and social figure,and his mother Bhuvaneshwari Devi ,was a devout housewife.

*Education: He studied Western logic, Western philosphy and European history at the general Assembly's Institution(now known as the Scottish Church College).

*Social Education:He believe that education was the primary means of rebuilding Indian society He thought that education should build character,instill human values, and make students self-reliant.

*Nationalism:He inspired many people to join the freedom struggle and work for nationalism.He believed that the best form of worship was service to the people.

*Author: He wrote many books such as 'Raja-Yoga','Karma Yoga:The Yoga of the Action','Meditation and its Methods','Juana-Yoga'.

*Introducing Indian philosophies to the west:He introduced Indian philosphies of Vendanta and Yoga to the West.

*Interfaith awareness:He opened up interfaith debates and awareness.

*Death: He passed away on 4 July 1902 .

*Philosophy:Swami Vivekananda's philosphy is based on the idea that humans are a combination of divinity,humanity,and animality..
